## 2026-10-27 - Linking Navigation Controls Programmatically
**Learning:** Adding `aria-expanded` and `aria-label` to toggle buttons (like hamburger menus or account dropdowns) is insufficient for full accessibility. Without `aria-controls` explicitly matching the target container's `id`, screen readers cannot programmatically associate the button with the menu it opens. Also, internal decorative SVGs within `aria-label`ed buttons should be hidden with `aria-hidden="true"` to prevent redundant reading.
**Action:** When creating or modifying toggle buttons (especially in headers/navbars), always ensure an explicit `id` is present on the target menu `div` or `nav`, and add a matching `aria-controls="[id]"` to the trigger button alongside `aria-expanded` and SVG `aria-hidden`.
